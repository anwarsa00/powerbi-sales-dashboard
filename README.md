📊 Power BI Dashboard – Analysis Summary
🎯 Overview

A Power BI dashboard was built to analyze sales and customer data, بهدف استخراج insights تساعد في فهم الأداء واتخاذ القرارات.

📄 Page 1: Overview
🎯 Objective:

Provide a quick overview of overall performance.

📌 KPIs:
Total Sales
Total Orders
Total Customers
Total Quantity
📊 Visuals:
Line Chart → Sales Over Time (overall trend)
Bar Chart → Sales by Category
Donut Chart → Sales Distribution (%)
Map → Sales by Country
📄 Page 2: Time Series Analysis
🎯 Objective:

Analyze sales performance over time.

📌 KPIs:
Total Sales
Max Monthly Sales
Min Monthly Sales
Avg Monthly Sales
📊 Visuals:
Column Chart → Monthly Sales Trend
🧠 Features:
Monthly and yearly analysis
Time-based sorting using Year-Month
Excluded incomplete months to improve accuracy
📄 Page 3: Customer Analysis
🎯 Objective:

Analyze customer behavior and segmentation.

🧠 Customer Segmentation:

Customers were segmented into:

VIP
Regular
New

Based on total spending.

📌 KPIs:
Total Customers
Avg Sales per Customer
VIP Customers
Customer Retention Rate
📊 Visuals:
Donut Chart → Customer Distribution by Segment (%)
Bar Chart → Sales by Customer Segment
Bar Chart → Customers by Country
Map → Sales by Country
Table → Top 10 Valuable Customers
🧠 Insights:
A large portion of customers are new
Regular customers generate the highest sales
VIP customers are fewer but high-value
📄 Page 4: Product Analysis
🎯 Objective:

Analyze product performance.

📌 KPIs:
Total Quantity
Total Products
Avg Sales per Product
Top Product Sales
📊 Visuals:
Bar Chart → Sales by Category
Donut Chart → Sales Distribution by Category (%)
Matrix → Sales by Category and Customer Segment
🧠 Insights:
Sales are highly concentrated in the Bikes category
New and Regular customers contribute the most
Accessories and Clothing show lower performance
🎨 Design & UX
Consistent color scheme across segments and categories
Logical layout (KPIs → Charts → Tables)
Used Top N filters for better clarity
Applied display units (K / M) for readability
🚀 Result

An interactive dashboard that provides:

Overall performance insights
Time-based trends
Customer behavior analysis
Product performance evaluation
